Why ENWT at CU Denver?

One excellent option to consider is CU Denver's English Writing, Rhetoric, and Technology (ENWT) degree. You can complete this degree online, on campus, or through a combination of both.

  • The same faculty who teach on campus also teach online — no robots teaching here!
  • Earn stackable, career-focused certificates along the way.
  • Build a professional portfolio that showcases your writing, digital, and AI-enhanced skills.

You can also earn certificates alongside the degree in areas such as Writing & Editing with AI, Technical & Professional Writing, Teaching English as a Second Language, Grant & Proposal Writing, and Narrative Nonfiction.

What you’ll learn

Skills that transfer across fields, roles, and technologies.

ENWT students learn to think critically, read carefully, solve problems creatively, and write with clarity, grace, and power—no matter the audience or medium.

  • ✓ Write effectively across contexts — workplace writing, digital storytelling, public-facing pieces, academic work, and multimodal composition.
  • ✓ Use emerging technologies strategically — including AI, collaborative platforms, and digital tools for research, drafting, and revision.
  • ✓ Communicate with diverse audiences — through inclusive, rhetorically aware writing that considers purpose, context, and community.
  • ✓ Build a professional identity — assemble a portfolio, practice project management, and learn the language of professional communication.

Flexible delivery

Designed for working students, transfers, and traditional undergraduates.

ENWT meets students where they are—geographically, professionally, and personally. You can complete the degree fully online, fully on campus, or by mixing course formats each semester.

Online

Study from anywhere

Take ENWT fully online while working, caregiving, or living outside the Denver metro area—without sacrificing access to CU Denver faculty and resources.

On campus

Urban campus experience

Learn in the heart of downtown Denver, connect with classmates and faculty in person, and take advantage of campus events, organizations, and resources.

Hybrid

Best of both worlds

Mix online and on-campus courses to fit changing work schedules and personal commitments—most ENWT students choose this flexible path.
