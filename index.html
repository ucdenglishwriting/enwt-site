Why ENWT at CU Denver?

An English degree designed for careers, not just classes.

ENWT helps you become the person organizations trust to write clearly, think critically, and communicate strategically—across platforms, audiences, and technologies.

Build a portfolio
Graduate with writing samples employers actually want to see: professional documents, digital projects, research-based writing, and multimodal work.
Write for real audiences
Practice genres used in workplaces and communities—so you can confidently apply your skills in internships, jobs, and graduate school.
Future-ready with AI
Learn to use AI ethically and effectively while sharpening the human skills that matter most: revision, style, persuasion, and judgment.
